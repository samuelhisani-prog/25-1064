TECHNOLOGIA....

Fairings

Fairings are streamlined structures designed to reduce aerodynamic drag by smoothing out irregular surfaces, gaps, and protrusions on vehicles like motorcycles. They improve speed, fuel efficiency, and performance.

Engines

These bikes have high-revving engines in various configurations, from singles to superbike-level four-cylinders. They are built for speed, agility, and performance.

Braking

Motorcycle braking systems are designed to convert kinetic energy into thermal energy through friction, with the front brake typically responsible for approximately 70% of the braking effort due to weight transfer during deceleration.

Body work and carbon fiber

Carbon fiber bodywork is a popular aftermarket upgrade for sport bikes, valued for its lightweight construction and high strength-to-weight ratio, which enhances performance and aerodynamics.
